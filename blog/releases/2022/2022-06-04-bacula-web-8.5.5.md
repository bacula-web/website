### Note

This release has been tested using PHP 7.3, 7.4.3 and 8.0 against

- Debian 9 (Stretch)
- Debian 10 (Buster)
- Ubuntu 18.10 (Cosmic Cuttlefish)
- Ubuntu 20.04 (Focal Fossa)
- Fedora 35
